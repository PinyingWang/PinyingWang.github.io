Crepe recipe

Things we need to prepare:

  • 125g flour
  • 250ml milk
  • 25g melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • A pinch of salt and vanilla

Then follow these steps:

  1. Mix everything with strong arms
  2. Put them in pan and flip them when there is some bubbles
  3. Add nutella, banana and Oreo cookies and honey
  4. Now it's time to enjoy and click on the picture
