Crepe recipe

Things we need to prepare:

  • 125g flour
  • 250ml milk
  • 25g melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • A pinch of salt and vanilla

Then follow these steps:

  • Mix everything with strong arms
  • Put them in pan and flip them when there is some bubbles
  • Add nutella, banana and Oreo cookies and honey
